# Writing Clearly and Concisely

Apply Strunk's timeless writing rules to technical documentation and agent communications.

## Overview

Applies "The Elements of Style" principles to technical writing: omit needless words, use active voice, prefer the specific over the general, avoid unnecessary qualifiers, and use strong verbs. Applied to agent response formatting, documentation, rule writing, and commit messages. Ensures clear communication without padding or hedging language.
